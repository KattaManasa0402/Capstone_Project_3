Problem Description

Many people, especially in the workplace, lack sufficient cybersecurity knowledge. Traditional training methods, such as reading lengthy documents or watching dull videos, often fail to engage learners. This results in poor retention and increases the risk of cyber-attacks. We aim to create an engaging and interactive way to educate people about cybersecurity, making the learning process both enjoyable and effective.

Objectives

  • Create a game with Unity to provide an interactive platform for learning cybersecurity.
  • Use Generative AI for dynamic Q&A creation, producing unique phishing or scam scenarios.
  • Implement a reward system with points, badges, and unlockable levels.
  • Offer real-time feedback to reinforce learning.
  • Track user progress for performance insights.

Background & Related Work

Previous cybersecurity training methods were repetitive and uninspiring. Gamification has proven to increase learner engagement. With Generative AI, our system can generate new scenarios on demand, providing a more realistic and adaptable training experience.

Technology Stack

  • Game Development: Unity (Personal Edition)
  • Backend: Python (Flask/FastAPI)
  • Generative AI: Llama 3 / Mistral
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Languages: Python 3.9+, C#

Innovation

Our project uniquely integrates Generative AI within a Unity game, ensuring that each playthrough offers fresh, relevant, and challenging content for users to engage with.
